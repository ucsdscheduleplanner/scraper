Academic Internship Program


Students must be matriculated UC San Diego undergraduates to enroll in Academic Internship Program courses, and meet eligibility requirements. Enrollment requires an approved AIP Learning Agreement and faculty advisor. Application details and deadlines are outlined on the AIP website: http://aip.ucsd.edu.
